The Development

A Boutique Collection in the Heart of Winchester

Set in the heart of historic Winchester, St George's House is a boutique collection of 9 beautifully finished apartments, designed for people who want city living without compromise.

Step outside and you are minutes from Winchester's independent shops, destination restaurants, bars, culture and theatres. Walk a little further and you are at the station, with direct rail services into London.

Inside, every home is specified to a high standard, with carefully considered layouts and a calm, contemporary finish. Selected apartments enjoy elevated views over Winchester's roofscape, with glimpses towards the cathedral.

Connected, Walkable, Effortless

A Serious Commuter Location

Winchester is a serious commuter location, without feeling like one. Fast rail services from Winchester connect you directly to London Waterloo, with the quickest journeys typically under an hour.

Local bus routes and city centre amenities are equally close, making day to day life genuinely walkable.

  • London Waterloo: Under 1 hour by train
  • Train Station: 10 min walk
  • High Street: 2 min walk
  • Winchester Cathedral: 5 min walk
